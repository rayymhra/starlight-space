Summary

Reid Wiseman is an American astronaut, engineer, and naval aviator who served as Flight Engineer for Expedition 41 aboard the International Space Station (ISS). During his mission, he contributed to various scientific research projects, performed a spacewalk, and shared his experiences with the public through social media, making space exploration more engaging for a global audience. His enthusiasm for space and dedication to scientific progress have made him a respected figure in the astronaut community.
