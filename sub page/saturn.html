Summary

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant known for its stunning ring system, which is made of ice and rock particles. Like Jupiter, Saturn is mostly composed of hydrogen and helium, with no solid surface. It has a strong magnetic field, numerous moons, and extreme atmospheric conditions.
