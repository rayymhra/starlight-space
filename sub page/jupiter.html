Summary

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant with a massive atmosphere composed mostly of hydrogen and helium. Known for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm that has raged for centuries, Jupiter has a strong magnetic field and dozens of moons. Its immense size and composition make it an important object of study for astronomers.
