A Decade of Global Precipitation

For ten years, the NASA-JAXA Global Precipitation Measurement (GPM) mission has tracked rain, snow, hurricanes, typhoons, and more. Launched in 2014 from Japan’s Tanegashima Space Center, the GPM Core Observatory was the first satellite to measure precipitation worldwide using radar.
