Summary

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to support life. It has a diverse environment with oceans, continents, and an atmosphere that sustains a wide variety of organisms. Earth’s unique conditions, including liquid water, a stable climate, and a protective magnetic field, make it habitable. It is the largest of the terrestrial planets and has a dynamic surface shaped by geological processes such as plate tectonics, erosion, and volcanic activity.
