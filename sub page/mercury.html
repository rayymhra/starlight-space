Summary

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest in the Solar System. Despite its proximity to the Sun, it is not the hottest planet. Mercury has a very thin atmosphere, extreme temperature fluctuations, and a heavily cratered surface similar to the Moon.
